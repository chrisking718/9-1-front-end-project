Highest Draw: High Aces!
Project using Deck of Cards API

Rules

  1. Add Player 1 Name. Click Submit
  2. Add Player 2 Name. Click Submit
  3. Click New Game Button
  4. Click Ok on Pop Up
  5. If you do not see an image, click the words, PLAYERCARD. This will show you the back of 4 cards
  6. Click on back of card to flip card over and reveal your card value
  7. When all 4 cards are revealed, click on add total
  8. The winner will appear at the top
  9. Click New Game to Start Over
